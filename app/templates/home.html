[Rules of the Game]

The objective of the game is to name as many Yu-Gi-Oh! cards as possible within a given time limit.
A player will be presented with a text input field where they can type the name of a Yu-Gi-Oh! card.
Upon typing the name of a card and pressing Enter, the card will be added to the list of validated cards if it exists.
Validated cards will be displayed on the screen.
If a card's name is not recognized or misspelled, it will not be added to the list and the input field may shake to indicate an error.
The game will continue until the time limit expires.
After the time limit, a modal will appear where the player can enter their username and submit their score.
The player's score is determined by the number of correctly named cards.
Once the score is submitted, the page may refresh to start a new game.
